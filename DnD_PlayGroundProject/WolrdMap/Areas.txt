Note1:
Every Area can be classified as: Forest, High Mountain, Desert, Volcanic etc...

Based on that classification, I can generate common themes for them. For example The Whispering Woods is a lvl 1-5 area of type Forest
This means that monsters are wolves, giant spiders, treants, bandits etc.... of lvl 1-5, they will drop herbs and ingredients etc...


Whenever there's an encounter, I will throw a d20 to check which monster it is according to the table below

Also, each zone will have its own modifiers. A Forest will have +2 Stealth for example.
